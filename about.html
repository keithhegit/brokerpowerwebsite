About Us

Broker Power was founded in 2014, providing high-performance IT consulting services, as well as value-added hardware and software solutions to our customers. Our expertise spans IT hardware infrastructure, big data, networking security, and cloud computing.

As a strategic and preferred partner to major IT brands such as Dell EMC, HP, Lenovo, Microsoft, Cisco, Juniper, and others, Broker Power has established a strong reputation for excellence in service.

We serve a diverse range of industries, including:

  • Finance
  • Education
  • Internet
  • Healthcare
  • Various other middle and small-sized enterprises

Our mission is to deliver high-quality IT solutions and consulting services that help our clients achieve their digital goals.
